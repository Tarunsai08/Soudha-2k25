About The Symposium

Welcome to SOUDHA 2K25

Crafting Your Vision

SOUDHA 2K25 is a premier national level technical symposium organized by the Department of Civil Engineering, JNTU Kakinada. This two-day event brings together brilliant minds from across the country to explore innovative ideas, showcase technical prowess, and foster collaboration among future engineers.

Through a series of competitions, workshops, and expert talks, participants will have the opportunity to enhance their skills, network with industry professionals, and gain valuable insights into the latest trends and technologies in civil engineering.
