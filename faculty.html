MAKE SURE TO REMEMBER

  • The Basic Registration Fee is mandatory to participate in Technical events and Workshops.

  • The Basic Registration Fee is not mandatory for Non Technical Events

  • And students who pay the basic registration fee are allowed to register for Accommodation and food.

  • We will provide an ID card required for fest entry in our college, for the students who pay the Basic Registration Fee.

  • Basic Registration is mandatory for all students, including team events.

  • We will accept Spots registrations also.

  • Please pay the fee correctly and co-operate with us.

  • For any queries for team registrations or events, please contact J. PAVAN KUMAR at 9347967873.

  • The Basic Registration Fee is mandatory to get access to pay fee for accommodation and food.

  • We will confirm the slots for the students who paid fee for accommodation and food along with basic registration fee.
